Hi!! I’m Brian Travis, a graduate of Forest Hills Central High School and an incoming freshman at the University of Michigan College of Engineering next fall. For as long as I can remember, I’ve loved tech, cameras, software engineering, hiking, nature, and traveling the world with my family, with some of my favorite places being Zion, Arches, Yosemite, and Glacier National Parks.

My mission is to provide a high quality portrait service that fits your personality and style, at a reasonable price, because photography is something that I love. If that sounds like something you’re interested in, great! I'm willing to take photos anywhere in or around the Grand Rapids area, but if you have another destination in mind, you can ask. For any question or inquiry, please do not hesitate to contact me through any of the mediums listed. I look forward to getting to know you!
